Business Intelligence Dashboard

An end-to-end BI solution with Snowflake data warehouse and Power BI dashboards for tracking warehouse compute costs and user activity patterns.

GitHub

Problem

Business teams lacked a unified and centralized view of key metrics. Data was siloed, making it difficult to analyze performance and make informed decisions, particularly regarding data warehouse costs and user activity.

Solution

I designed and implemented a comprehensive end-to-end business intelligence solution. This involved building a data warehouse in Snowflake based on a star schema model, where I created fact and dimension tables. I then leveraged Power BI to connect to this warehouse, perform data transformations with Power Query, and create dynamic, interactive dashboards.

Key Insights

The dashboards provided critical insights into the company's warehouse compute costs and user activity patterns. This visibility helped identify underutilized resources and optimize data operations, directly impacting the bottom line.

How It Works

Data was loaded into Snowflake and structured into a star schema for efficient querying. I used SQL and DAX to create custom measures and calculated columns in Power BI, powering the visualizations. The dashboards featured various views, enabling teams to drill down into specific metrics and understand performance at a granular level.

Impact

  • Optimized Performance: Enabled key stakeholders to analyze and reduce compute costs, improving overall data efficiency.
  • Improved Decision-Making: Provided cross-functional teams with a single source of truth for key metrics, allowing for more data-driven and informed strategic decisions.
